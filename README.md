# MALFI
### Make A List For It!

That's basically all this project will be - a place where anyone can create, update and complete lists of TODOs.

In times like these having a structure to your life really matters. Ticking off items as `complete` gives you a mental boost, making you feel like you've accomplished something, even if it's just the tiniest of things. With this project I want to try to contribute to our mental well-being through bringing to life this alternative way of structuring your day!
