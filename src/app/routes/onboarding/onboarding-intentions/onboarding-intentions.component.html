Intentions

Intentions can be thought of as commitments to specific tasks. You set a time and date on which you want to work on the task and can add optional notes. Intentions are always linked to a task and cannot be defined without selecting one from a Goal.


The intentions including their dates will be shown in the calendar. If you already use a different calendar, you may also export the intentions to a .ics-file and import them to a calendar of your choice.
